What is Warriors all about

Where it all started from

Once there lived a cat named Rusty who lived with his human owner who did not care about him. One time at the day of a full moon he has a dream about hunting a mouse. That dream causes him to crave a fresh killed mouse so in the morning rusty is preparing to leave for the forest a other cat who lived next door asked rusty where he was going rusty tells him that he his leaving for the forest to go eat a mouse the other cat warns Rusty about the dangers of the forest but Rusty does not cares and steps into the forest. After a while Rusty hears something in the bushes before he could react a gray cat unleashes a powerful attack at Rusty after Rusty fights backs fiercefully 2 other cat appear from the bushes it then that Rusty learns about the 4 mighty clan that live in the forest. The next thing Rusty hears is a complimenting meow from the thunderclan leader BlueStar and an offer to join the thunder clan.
